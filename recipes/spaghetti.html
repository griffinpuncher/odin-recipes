Spaghetti

Description

This simple spaghetti recipe will have your mouth watering by the time you're done cooking! Make sure not to skimp on quality ingredients, the fresher the better.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Meat Balls
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Simmer meatballs in marinara sauce until cooked thoroughly.
  2. Boil pasta in salted water.
  3. Drain pasta, keeping a cup of starchy water for later.
  4. Combine pasta, sauce, and starchy water over medium heat. Stir together for 30 seconds, the turn stove off.
  5. Mix in butter, this will help your pasta not stick.
