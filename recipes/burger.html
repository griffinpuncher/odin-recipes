Burger

Description

This simple burger recipe will have your mouth watering by the time you're done cooking! Make sure not to skimp on quality ingredients, the fresher the better.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • White Onion
  • Tomato
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Mince half of the onion.
  2. Combine the ground beef, onion, and cornstarch. Make sure to knead the onion bits into the meat thoroughly.
  3. Form into patties, cook to medium.
  4. Off heat, place cheese of choice on the patties and let it melt.
  5. Toast the buns.
  6. Assemble the burger.
